Never Empty: 3 Day Startup Company

During Fall 2014, four others and I created Never Empty: a gasoline delivery company. The idea was to partner with companies to provide their employees with gas refills in company parking lots. After 3 Day Startup, we entered our business plan into the Texas Venture Labs Investment Competition where we were semifinalists. We pursued the idea for some time afterward, but ultimately the idea was put on hold indefinitely.
